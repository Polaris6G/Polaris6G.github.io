1.职业Occupation

提问职业：

  • What do you do?
  • What kind/sort of work do you do?
  • What line of work are you in?
  • What do you do for a living?

职业形式口语：

  • self-employed 自营业
  • salesperson 营销人员
  • do a voluntary job/social service in an non-profit organization
  • part-timer 兼职
  • freelancer 自由职业者
  • on a break/sabbatical产假
  • nine to five 朝九晚五
  • regular work hours
  • clock in 上班打卡 clock out 下班打卡
  • in shifts 倒班

单词短语认知：

  • internship 实习
  • we are from all works of life 各行各业
  • She is a bread winner 养家糊口
  • work for government 公务员
  • trainee 培训师
  • benefit 奖金，福利
  • office building 写字楼
  • stamina 耐力，毅力
  • exert 努力，尽力
  • AKA = also known as
  • receptive 乐于接受得，愿意倾听的
  • dictate 意味着
  • It’s really pay off 很值得

2. 公司Company

section

  • Human resources 人力资源部门
  • Sales 销售部门
  • Production 产品部门
  • Finance 财政部门
  • Marketing 营销部门
  • Purchasing 采购部门
  • Public relation 公共关系部门
  • After-sales service 售后服务部门
  • Quality assurance 质检部门
  • General affairs 常务部门（后勤）
  • Administrative office 行政部门
  • Logistics 物流部门

Types

  • state-owned enterprise 国企

  • family-owned enterprise 家族企业

  • listed/quoted company 上市企业

  • privately-owned enterprise 私企

  • Sino-foreign joint venture 中外合资企业

  • multinational corporation 跨国公司

  • group corporation 集团公司

relation

branches 分公司

outlets 专营店，经销店，卖场

subsidiary 子公司

head office 总公司

术语

turnover 销售额

share price 股票价格

profit 盈利

workforce 劳动力

单词短语认知：

  • law firm 律师事务所
  • warehouse 仓库
  • call center
  • distribution center
  • service center
  • consignment 货物
  • time-honored 前瞻的
  • newly established company
  • perk 额外收入 津贴 特殊待遇 好处
  • measuring performance 绩效考核
  • accountable 追究责任的
  • hot desk 公用办公桌
  • five-star hotel
  • We haven’t seen for ages
  • oversee 审查
  • approve 审批
  • regional 其余的
  • net profit 净利润
  • plunge 跳水
  • highly competitive
  • flat sell 销售不景气
  • segment 划分
  • out of hand 无法控制；托收；告终；立即
  • cough up 咳出；勉强说出；被迫付出
  • breach of contract 违约；违反合同
  • fine print （合同、协议的）附属细则，小字
  • initial v. 签名（姓名首字母）n. 首字母 a. 最初的，开始的，第一的
  • future-oriented 面向未来的
  • customer-driven 客户导向的
  • values-driven 价值导向的

优美句子：

it offers flexible work schedules, compressed work weeks,and a generous telecommuting policy——as well as a gym with two personal trainers and an on-site car wash, of course.

In Microsoft, employees enjoy a range of legendary including dry cleaning, a Wifi-enabled shuttle service,and valet parking at some buildings.

Women hold more than 30% of the Top 500 positions at Amex, high for financial services. ALL employees have access to on-site medical service as well as a “health navigation concierge to help” maximize their benefits.

翻译：

我在华为公司的销售部门工作已经快十年了。这是国内非常有名的私企，而且是一家跨国公司。虽然每天我都要打卡上下班，但是工作给我带来了很大的满足感。公司的领导相信自主性，也关注责任感，并且十分注重业绩评估。值得一提的是，华为的福利待遇很好，我们可以享受弹性工作制，上班时间是可以压缩的，只要项目做完就好，并且还可以远程办公。在华为工作真是一件幸福的事情

I have been working in the Sales Department of Huawei for nearly ten years. This is a very famous private enterprise in China, and it is a multinational company. Although I have to clock in and out every day, work has brought me great satisfaction. The leaders of the company believe in autonomy, also pay attention to the sense of responsibility, and attach great importance to performance evaluation. It is worth mentioning that Huawei has very good welfare benefits. We can enjoy a flexible working schedules. Working hours can be compressed. As long as the project is completed, we can also work in telecommuting. Some happy to work in Huawei.

5. memo and minutes

  • What is memo and mimutes？

  • What is the purpose of writing them?

  • How to write them?

构成

minutes：（会议记录）

venue 场所，发生地点

present 到场人

apologies for absence 缺席人

new proposals 新提案

resolution 决议

adjournment 休会

memo：（便函）

To

From

Date

Subject

（content）body goes here~

场景模拟

A： Good morning,since everyone is here， Let’s get started . The purpose of our meeting today is to discuss the new Department Manager and the arrangements for the May Day holiday. OK, let’s start with item one.

B: I think Andy is the right person for a marketing manager. It has been doing well in our company for five years and has strong customer communication skills.

C: I think that Andy is over confident, not fit to be the leader.

A: What do you think,Daniel?

D: As far as I know, he’s not welcomed with the team at all.

B: But Andy raised the annual turnover by 20%. I think you’re off the point.

A: Can you let him finish, bill? No matter how strong a person’s working ability is, it’s difficult to be a good leader if he can’t bear the weight（服众）.

C: exactly, we need to spend more time watching him.

A: If no one has supplement, let’s move to the next topic.
